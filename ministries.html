Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry is designed for young people up to 25 years old, fostering spiritual growth and community.

We offer a vibrant Children's Choir for younger participants to engage in worship through music and praise.

Additionally, our Youth Choir gathers once a month for rehearsal to sharpen their talents and glorify God together.

Women's Ministry - D.O.V.E

D.O.V.E stands for Daughters of Virtue and Excellence, empowering women to walk boldly in faith and leadership.

We host quarterly events to nurture fellowship, growth, and sisterhood.

Women's Day Services throughout the year celebrate and honor the vital role of women in the Kingdom.

Through worship, teaching, and connection, women are equipped to live with purpose and excellence.

Men's Ministry

Equipping men to be strong leaders in their families, church, and communities.

Mother's Board

The Mother's Board is a dedicated group of senior women who provide spiritual wisdom, mentorship, and prayer support to the church community.

They meet monthly to fellowship, pray, and offer guidance rooted in faith and experience.

These women are pivotal to the strength and legacy of our church, embodying grace, leadership, and unwavering commitment.
